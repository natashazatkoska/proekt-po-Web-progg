Natasha Zatkoska

healthy food & design is my passion

I am Natasha Zatkoska, a 24 year old girl with a huge passion for healthy food, fitness and everything related to a healthy lifestyle.

My desire is to start implementing as many healthy habits as possible . From there my Instagram profile started to develop, and after a while it grew into a blog like this where you will find everything from my recepies in one place. My goal is to show how you can build healthy habits without restrictions and strict diets and to capture how much food and a balanced life help us to be the best version of ourselves, while not missing those good things in life.

Also I'am very creative person , so i love fashion and design as well . I will share my favourite designs here , hope you will enjoy :)
